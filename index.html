More Than a Retreat

This is about discovering your calling as a God-Bearer and living it boldly

Join us for the 2026 Byzantine Catholic Young Adult Convocation at St. Mary Byzantine Catholic Church in Hillsborough, NJ. Together we will explore what it means to allow Mary's monastic heart to be our guide as Eastern Christians, whatever our state in life.

"Let it be done unto me according to your word." -Luke 1:38

Anchored through liturgical prayer, we will discover how the ideal of monasticism, following after the Theotokos, provides the roadmap for living poverty, chastity, and obedience as the universal Christian call to detachment, wholeness, and surrender.

Three Days of Transformation

Built around liturgical prayer, the heartbeat of monastic life

Every element flows from the rhythm of liturgical prayer. We begin and end each day with the hours, forming the backbone of our retreat, supplemented by talks from our speakers and time together in fellowship.

The Dormition Fast setting adds spiritual resonance as we prepare alongside the Church for celebrating Mary's dormition.

Three Pillars

Liturgical Hours

Pray with the Church with 3rd, 6th, and 9th hours, Vespers, and Compline forming the core of our weekend.

Formation Talks

Three substantial talks exploring the Theotokos as the model monastic heart and your calling to be a God-bearer in the world.

Community

Connect with like-minded young adults from across the country who share your desire for deeper faith and authentic Christian community.

Meet Your Speakers

Amazing speakers coming soon!

We're putting together an incredible lineup of speakers who bring the fruits of their prayer and teach us how monastic spirituality can be lived in any state of life.

Get ready for talks on:

  • The Theotokos as the ultimate model of monastic surrender
  • Living evangelical poverty, chastity, and obedience outside the monastery
  • Your universal call to be a God-bearer and evangelist
  • Practical wisdom for authentic witness in today's world
